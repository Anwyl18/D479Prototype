Featuring Manava Suite Resort Tahiti and Vahine Private Island Resort

Escape to a private islet in Taha'a and experience the ultimate in romantic seclusion. Includes round-trip international air from Los Angeles, all transfers and inter-island air, one night at Manava Suite Resort Tahiti, five nights in a beach bungalow at Vahine Private Island on Taha'a, daily breakfast and dinner for two on Taha'a, complimentary use of water sports equipment, and more. 

*Note: Boat transfers from Raiatea Airport to Vahine Island are not permitted after 4PM for navigational safety reasons. It is strongly advised all guests plan to arrive in Raiatea on a morning flight.

What's Included

Taha'a Resort

5 nights

Vahine Island Resort & Spa

Vahine Island is located on a small private islet along the northeastern coral reef surrounding Taha'a. Only thirty minutes away by boat from Raiatea, this prime placement delivers scenic views of Taha'a, Raiatea, Huahine and Bora Bora. Tucked inside a pristine coconut grove, this intimate boutique hotel provides the ultimate privacy and blends seamlessly with the island's natural environment.

ROOMS

← →

Included

Beach Bungalow

The Beach Bungalow overlooks the lagoon and the island of Bora Bora from a spacious deck complete with a hammock and outdoor shower. It can comfortably accommodate a king bed or two twin beds upon request.

Upgrade from $360pp

Deluxe Beach Suite

The Deluxe Beach Suite is truly unique with two separate decks overlooking both the garden and the lagoon. The bathroom features double sinks and a shower, while the living room provides a sofa and two armchairs with a DVD player and LCD screen.

Upgrade from $465pp

Overwater Bungalow

The spacious Overwater Bungalow offers direct access to the lagoon. The bedroom can easily accommodate a king bed or two twin beds upon request and the bathroom comes equipped with double sinks. The sitting area features an aquarium table with a view into the lagoon. Guests must be 12 years of age or older.

Tahiti Resort

1 night

Te Moana Tahiti Resort

The Te Moana Tahiti Resort is a modern hotel that rests alongside the water and provides scenic views of Moorea across the lagoon. Located on the western coast, this hotel is only ten minutes from the airport. The rooms feature their own kitchenette and complimentary Wi-Fi, making this a popular choice for families and business travelers. With the largest infinity pool on the island, this is the ultimate place to unwind after your flight.
